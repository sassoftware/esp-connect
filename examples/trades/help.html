This example pushes stock trade data through an ESP model looking for trade violations.

The violation data displayed includes the following:
  • Gauges displaying the total amount of violations committed by each broker.
  • A map and table showing the amount of violations occurring at each trading venue.
  • A table showing min, max, and average trade prices for high volume symbols (over 100 trades).
  • A time series chart showing the event processing rates for both total and interval.
  • Gauges showing total events processed and current interval rate.
  • Gauges displaying the amount of violations by type.
    • When you load the page, it will load the project into the ESP server. Upon a successful load, a publisher will read the 1 million trades from trades.txt and send them to the server.

    Since the page is both sending 1 million events and processing the results it is very busy. The event rates will fluctuate with the heavy processing over the million events.
